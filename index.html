ABOUT US

Security tailored to you.
Designed to stop real threats.

We are a company specialized in Information Security, with solid experience in implementation, audits, and cybersecurity training. Our goal is to help organizations identify, mitigate, and prevent vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

Services

We offer specialized services and tailored solutions

🛡️ Cybersecurity Workshops

Practical training that teaches users how to face current threats. By training employees, mistakes that could trigger incidents such as ransomware attacks are reduced, protecting critical business information.

🎣 Phishing Attack Simulations

Phishing is the main infiltration method in companies due to the human factor. We run customized phishing campaigns, assess user susceptibility, and provide follow-up training to reinforce best practices.

🔍 IT Systems Audit (On-Premise and Cloud)

Includes vulnerability analysis, penetration testing, application auditing, and firewall hardening. We identify gaps, assess risks, and deliver a report with recommendations to protect your infrastructure.

🖥️ Device Protection (Cloud and On-Premise)

Installation and configuration of next-generation antivirus solutions with protection against malware, ransomware, exploits, and zero-day attacks. We guide you in cloud management and the creation of strong security policies.

☁️ Backup and Disaster Recovery Plans (DRP)

Your information is your most valuable asset. We implement cloud backup and recovery solutions to ensure business continuity in the face of any eventuality.

📦 Cybersecurity Portfolio for SMEs

If you do not know where to start, we support you from the initial advisory stage to full implementation: vulnerability analysis, hardening, data protection, backup programs, and training for your technical team.
